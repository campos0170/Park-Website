About Us

Doral regional park is located in the heart of south Florida. For over 50 years it has been dedicated to the perservation and cultivation of nature as well as providing an enviroment were visotors can feel in contact with nature. We operate under the belief that humanity belongs to nature and nature to humanity. We are dedicated to providing an environment were visitors can experience a multitude of experiences, from a quiet afternoon reading, to their most memorable celebrations.
